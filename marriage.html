Marriage Certificate Attestation

Marriage certificate attestation is a process in which the marriage certificate is verified for international usage. Any married individual has to do marriage certificate attestation if he/she is traveling to a foreign country. It is important to do marriage certificate attestation to prove people's relationship status internationally. A marriage certificate is a vital record that may affirm that the people referenced in the certificate are legitimately married. The certificate is acquired from the concerned government authority. Details regarding the couple will be mentioned in the certificate as the name, age, etc. of the couple and place, date of the marriage, etc. The marriage certificate attestation procedure helps migrate couples living in foreign countries, which is globally used. Marriage certificate attestation helps an individual in many ways. There are different rules in foreign countries.

Main Purposes of Marriage Certificate Attestation:

  • For employment.
  • For resident visa.
  • For education purposes.
  • To attain a dependent visa.
  • To take family abroad.

    • Marriage certificate attestation comes under non-educational certificate attestation. There are various purposes for attaining a marriage certificate attestation. It mainly depends on the rules in the international countries. The procedure of marriage certificate attestation has to be seriously followed. It is very important to get the marriage certificate attested by all the authorities required.

    Documents Required for Marriage Certificate Attestation:

  • Original certificate.
  • Passport copy.

    • These documents are very much important and necessary for marriage certificate attestation. Documents must be original for them to be considered for the attestation procedures.

    Procedures of Marriage Certificate Attestation:

  • Notary attestation.
  • Home department attestation.
  • MEA (Ministry of External Affairs) attestation.
  • Embassy attestation.
  • MOFA (Ministry of Foreign Affairs) attestation.

    • The above mentioned are the important procedures to be completed for marriage certificate attestation. The documents required for marriage certificate attestation are sealed and signed by the concerned authorities. A marriage certificate is a document that acts as legal proof of the marriage between two individuals. This certificate has all the details of the two individuals married. Marriage certificate attestation increases the credibility of the certificate for international usage. It helps the couples show their marriage's realness before the concerned immigration officials. If someone got a job or anything in an international country and he/she wants to take their partner with them, this is where marriage certificate attestation is required and necessary.

    Marriage certificate attestation is a requirement for the destination country to ensure safety from illegal documentation. The marriage certificate has to be confirmed authentic before using it worldwide, so attestation is important. Successful completion of marriage certificate attestation lets individuals travel to any country without legal issues. The procedures of marriage certificate attestation are very important and have to be followed correctly. Marriage certificate attestation becomes mandatory when a couple or family travels to foreign countries for different purposes.
