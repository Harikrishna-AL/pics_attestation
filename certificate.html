Certificate Attestation

Degree certificate attestation is the process of making degree certificate authentic by verifying it under a government authority. Degree certificate is attained by a person when he/she completes graduation from a university. The degree certificate is issued by the concerned university or college. It is important to attain the degree certificate from the university itself. Degree certificate is the basic document for higher education and employment. Degree certificate is to show the academic qualification of an individual. If anyone who wishes to travel to foreign countries for any purposes, he/she has to do the attestation procedure. Degree certificate attestation is done when a person wants to travel foreign countries for various purposes. Degree certificate is an important certificate for any immigrant. Degree certificate attestation helps the individual attain various career objectives.
Various purposes of degree certificate attestation:

  • For employment purposes
  • For higher education purposes
  • For migration purposes
  • For obtaining residence visa

    • There are various reasons people tend to get degree certificate attestation. It is mandatory to do degree certificate attestation for attaining higher education or jobs in foreign countries. After doing degree certificate attestation the value of the certificate in the destination country increases. All the documents have to be submitted by the applicant without any fail.

    Documents required for degree certificate attestation:
  • Original degree certificate
  • Passport copy

    • There are several procedures for completing the process of degree certificate attestation. All the steps have much importance.

    Procedures of degree certificate attestation:
  • SDM (sub-divisional magistrate)/ HRD (human resource development).
  • MEA (ministry of external affairs).
  • Embassy attestation.

    • The documents have to be sealed and signed from the above mentioned authorities for the certificate to be considered verified. Degree certificate attestation beholds a very important part in ones career building. Now-a-days every country requires this legalisation for ensuring the trustworthiness of the outside document. It ensures safety from illegal documentation to the destination country. More international opportunities are provided by the certification. Attestation of certificates will avoid legal complications in your further dealings and it will enhance the communication with destination country without any legal barriers. Attestation is very important while acquiring visa for the destination country. Degree certificate attestation is also very much important for visa purposes. It is important to make sure that the documents carried by the immigrant are attested. Original certificates can only undergo the process of degree certificate attestation. Degree certificate attestation plays a major role building up the career of a person.

    People travel without limitations in this globalised world. People tend to move to foreign countries more often foe various purposes. Migration has become very common these days that degree certificate attestation is an ongoing procedure. For your degree certificate to be used in other than your home country, degree certificate attestation needs to be done.

    Main aim of degree certificate attestation is to prove that the individual has completed the particular qualification and also to shoe that the documents submitted are genuine.
