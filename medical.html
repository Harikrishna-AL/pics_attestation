Medical Certificate Attestation

A medical certificate is a written certificate from a doctor after examining a patient. It is evidence of a health condition. A medical certificate is necessary when your leave exceeds a certain limit. Attesting a medical certificate is necessary to claim insurance, to get leave extension, etc., in a foreign country. Medical certificate attestation is a prominent attestation type that comes under the category of non-educational certificate attestation. The medical certificate attestation is done on medical certificates to prove its authenticity. It is a procedure in which the signature or seal of the authority on the medical certificate is made to be used in countries overseas. The seal and signature on the certificate show that the respective department has verified it and is genuine.

Medical certificate attestation helps you to get health benefits, and it also serves as an authentic and legal document if you want to submit proof for your absence in the office. The process of medical certificate attestation has many phases. It is also necessary that you should present some documents before the authority for attestation.

Documents Required:

  • Original medical certificate
  • Passport Copy
  • 2 Passport Size Photos

The procedure of authentication of a medical certificate is termed as medical certificate attestation. The process of medical certificate attestation is different for different countries. The procedures may differ depending upon the country you wish to use the certificate. Before submitting the medical certificate before any office, legalizing the medical certificate is necessary. The need for medical certificate attestation is to get your leave extended, proving that the absence was due to a medical issue. The attestation is only done if the certificate is issued by a doctor or a medical practitioner. Thus, medical certificate attestation is the act of legalizing a medical certificate issued by a doctor.
