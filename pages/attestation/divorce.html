The Attestation of Divorce Certificates falls under the non-educational certificate attestation category. A divorce certificate is required by certain foreign organisations. A divorce certificate is the official court document that ends a marriage. The purpose of divorce certificate attestation is to raise the validity and credibility of the document.

Divorce certificates that have been attested are recognised globally as genuine documents. All of the attestation procedure's techniques will be implemented by the appropriate authorities.

The official seal or signature is embossed on the paperwork, making the certificate more easily accessible in the intended nation. All of the divorce's related information is contained on the certificate. The attestation of a divorce certificate requires the completion of multiple steps.

Steps involved in attesting a divorce certificate:

  • Notary attestation.
  • MEA (ministry of external affairs) attestation.
  • Embassy attestation.
  • MOFA (ministry of foreign affairs) attestation.

    • Required documents for divorce certificate attestation:

  • Original Divorce Certificate.
  • A copy of the applicant's passport.
