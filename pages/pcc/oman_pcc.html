Oman PCC

Oman is a traditional nation in the Arab world and one of the earliest independent states in the region. The government of Oman provides residents and immigrants with a police certification known as an Oman Police Clearance Certificate (PCC) upon their request.

Oman Police Clearance Certificate (PCC)

A police clearance certificate is the only document that demonstrates your lack of involvement in any criminal history. If you have a criminal record, document attestation is not possible. Therefore, the PCC is the sole evidence that can attest to your sincere character.

Individuals with a Police Clearance Certificate (PCC) have never been involved in any criminal activities. You can obtain this certificate if you are older than 18, have ever traveled to Oman from the UAE, or have worked in Oman for more than six months.

The Oman Police Clearance Certificate guarantees the applicant's lack of criminal history within the nation's borders.
